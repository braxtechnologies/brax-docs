# K-POC

**K-POC (Knowledge and Personal Objective Cycle) is integral to brax performance** evaluation as it aligns individual development goals with organizational objectives. By focusing on knowledge acquisition and personal growth, **K-POC ensures that team members enhance their skills in areas critical to brax's performance metrics**, thereby driving overall productivity and success.
