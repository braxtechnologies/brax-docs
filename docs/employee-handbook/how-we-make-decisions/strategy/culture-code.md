## When Culture Alignment Falters

We recognize that people are human. Even dedicated team members can occasionally lose focus, and sometimes culture mismatches slip through the hiring process. Technical excellence alone isn't enough‚Äîcultural fit matters equally.

**We believe in second chances.** People can change, and we've developed the [Corrective Action Plan (CAP)](../../how-we-ensure-peoples-growth/performance/cap.md) process to address culture misalignment constructively.

### How the CAP Works

The CAP provides a structured opportunity for realignment:

1. **Clarity** - We clearly identify the specific behaviors and attitudes that need improvement
2. **Ownership** - The employee creates their own action plan for change
3. **Reflection** - The process offers space to reflect on whether the role is the right fit
4. **Support** - We provide guidance and time to demonstrate change

This gives everyone a fair chance to realign with our culture before taking further measures.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** While we believe in growth and second chances, our Permission to Play values are non-negotiable. The CAP is an opportunity to course-correct, but continued misalignment with our core values will result in separation from the company.
{% endhint %}
