# BraX3 Repair Guide

Welcome to the BraX3 repair guide. This section provides instructions for common repairs and maintenance procedures for your BraX3 device.

### Screen Replacement
Instructions for replacing a damaged or cracked screen.

### Battery Replacement
Step-by-step guide for replacing the battery.

### Charging Port Repair
Guide for fixing or replacing the USB-C charging port.

### Button Replacement
Instructions for replacing power, volume, or other physical buttons.

## Tools Required

Before attempting any repairs, ensure you have the proper tools:

- Phillips #00 screwdriver
- Plastic opening tools
- Suction cup
- Tweezers
- Heat gun or hair dryer (for screen removal)
- Spudger tool

## Safety Warnings

{% include alerts/warning.html content="Performing repairs on your device may void your warranty. Always power off your device before attempting any repair. Work in a clean, well-lit area and keep track of all screws and components." %}

## Before You Begin

1. **Back up your data** - Always back up important data before performing repairs
2. **Power off the device** - Ensure the device is completely powered off
3. **Discharge static electricity** - Use an anti-static wrist strap if available
4. **Organize your workspace** - Keep screws and small parts organized
