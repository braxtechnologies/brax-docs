## Overview

The Brax Technologies Technical Partner Program connects innovative companies, developers, and organizations to collaborate on open source mobile technology. Our program fosters a ecosystem of technical excellence, shared knowledge, and mutual growth.

## Why Join?

### For Partners

- **Early Access**: Get early access to new devices, features, and technical specifications
- **Technical Support**: Direct communication channels with Brax engineering teams
- **Co-Development**: Collaborate on OS ports, device drivers, and custom solutions
- **Marketing Support**: Joint marketing opportunities and co-branding initiatives
- **Community Recognition**: Featured partner status in our community and documentation

### For the Ecosystem

- **Expanded Device Support**: More OS options and customizations for end users
- **Innovation**: Collaborative R&D pushing mobile technology boundaries
- **Quality**: Rigorous testing and validation through partner networks
- **Sustainability**: Extended device lifecycles through diverse software options

## Application Process

### Step 1: Submit Application

Complete our partner application form with:

- Organization details and background
- Partnership goals and objectives
- Technical capabilities and experience
- Proposed collaboration areas
- Expected timeline and milestones

### Step 2: Technical Review

Our partnership team reviews:

- Technical feasibility
- Alignment with Brax values and goals
- Resource requirements
- Potential impact on ecosystem
- Legal and compliance considerations

### Step 3: Agreement

For approved partners:

- Partnership agreement signed
- Technical onboarding completed
- Access credentials provided
- Kickoff meeting scheduled
- Success metrics defined

### Step 4: Activation

- Partner portal access
- Technical documentation delivered
- Hardware samples shipped (if applicable)
- Joint announcement prepared
- Regular check-ins scheduled

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a cost to join the partner program?**
A: Community Partner tier is free. Technical and Strategic tiers may involve shared costs for hardware samples and custom development.

**Q: What if I'm an individual developer?**
A: Individual developers are welcome to apply! We evaluate based on technical merit and project impact, not organization size.

**Q: Can I partner on multiple areas?**
A: Yes! Many partners work across OS development, apps, and hardware integration.

**Q: How long does the application process take?**
A: Typically 2-4 weeks from application to approval, depending on complexity.

**Q: What are the minimum requirements?**
A: Demonstrated technical capability, alignment with open source values, and commitment to quality and community standards.
