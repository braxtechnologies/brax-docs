# Social Impact

## Our Commitment to Social Responsibility

At Brax Technologies, we believe technology should empower everyone, not just the privileged few. We are dedicated to creating positive social change through inclusive design, ethical practices, and community engagement.

![](/docs/assets/images/opensource-experience-logo.png)

## Core Principles

### Digital Inclusion

* **Affordable Technology**: Making quality devices accessible to underserved communities
* **Open Source Software**: Free, transparent alternatives to proprietary systems
* **Right to Repair**: Empowering users to maintain and extend device lifespans
* **Accessibility Features**: Inclusive design for users of all abilities

### Privacy & User Rights

* **Privacy by Design**: Protecting user data as a fundamental right
* **Transparency**: Clear communication about data collection and usage
* **User Control**: Giving individuals ownership of their digital lives
* **No Surveillance Capitalism**: Rejecting business models built on user exploitation

### Community Empowerment

* **Open Documentation**: Free access to repair guides and technical resources
* **Skills Development**: Training programs for repair technicians and developers
* **Local Partnerships**: Supporting community repair shops and maker spaces
* **Knowledge Sharing**: Contributing to global open source communities

### Ethical Labor Practices

* **Fair Wages**: Ensuring livable compensation throughout our supply chain
* **Safe Working Conditions**: Partnering only with ethical manufacturers
* **Worker Rights**: Supporting freedom of association and collective bargaining
* **Transparent Auditing**: Regular third-party assessments of labor practices

## Impact Areas

### Education & Skills

* Free technical training programs
* Scholarship opportunities for underrepresented groups in tech
* Educational device donation programs
* Open source curriculum development

### Economic Opportunity

* Job creation through repair and refurbishment networks
* Supporting small business repair shops

* Fair pricing models that respect economic diversity

### Health & Safety

* Reducing exposure to hazardous materials
* Promoting mental health through ethical design
* Supporting digital wellbeing initiatives
* Reducing electromagnetic exposure through efficient design

### Digital Rights

* Advocacy for right to repair legislation
* Support for data privacy regulations
* Fighting planned obsolescence
* Promoting software freedom

## Measuring Our Impact

We track and report on:

* Number of individuals gaining device access through affordability programs
* Community repair workshops and participants
* Open source contributions and collaborators
* Supply chain labor practice audits
* User privacy and security metrics

## Partnerships & Collaborations

We work with:

* Non-profit organizations focused on digital equity
* Educational institutions and vocational programs
* Community repair networks and maker spaces
* Human rights and labor advocacy groups
* Open source foundations and projects
