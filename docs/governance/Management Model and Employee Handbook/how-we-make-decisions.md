# How We Make Decisions

## Decision-Making Framework

At Lunr, we believe in empowering team members to make decisions while ensuring alignment with our mission and values.

## Principles

### Transparency
All decisions should be made with clear reasoning and communicated openly to affected stakeholders.

### Data-Driven
We base decisions on evidence, metrics, and insights whenever possible.

### Collaborative
Key decisions involve input from relevant team members and stakeholders.

### Accountable
Decision-makers are responsible for outcomes and learning from results.

### Aligned
All decisions should support our vision, mission, and strategic objectives.

## Decision Levels

### Individual Decisions
Team members are empowered to make decisions within their area of responsibility.

### Team Decisions
Collaborative decisions affecting team operations and deliverables.

### Leadership Decisions
Strategic decisions affecting company direction and major initiatives.

## Process

1. **Identify the Decision**: Clearly define what needs to be decided
2. **Gather Information**: Collect relevant data and perspectives
3. **Consider Options**: Evaluate alternatives against our values and goals
4. **Make the Decision**: Choose the best path forward
5. **Communicate**: Share the decision and rationale with stakeholders
6. **Execute**: Implement the decision
7. **Review**: Assess outcomes and learn from results
