# Business Unit

## Overview

The Brax ecosystem consists of multiple business units working collaboratively to advance our mission of privacy-first, open-source technology. Each business unit operates independently while sharing our core values and commitment to user freedom.

## Our Business Units

### [Brax Technologies](BraxTechnologies.md)
The flagship company focused on developing privacy-respecting hardware and software solutions for mobile devices.

**Focus Areas:**
- Mobile device development (BraX3)
- Operating system development (BraxOS)
- Privacy-first applications
- Open-source ecosystem

---

### [Lunr Technologies](Lunr Technologies.md)
Dedicated to advancing mobile operating systems and custom ROM development.

**Focus Areas:**
- Custom ROM development (LunarOS)
- Mobile Device Management-MDM (Lunar Control Center)
- Developer tools
- The Lunar Network

---

## Collaboration

Our business units work together to:

- **Share Resources**: Technical expertise, infrastructure, and knowledge
- **Coordinate Development**: Aligned roadmaps and compatible technologies
- **Support Community**: Joint community initiatives and open-source contributions
- **Maintain Standards**: Consistent quality and privacy standards across all products

## Governance Structure

Each business unit maintains operational independence while adhering to:

- Shared [Code of Conduct](../code-of-conduct/index.md)
- Common [Environmental Impact](../Environmental Impact/index.md) goals
- Unified [Social Impact](../Social Impact/index.md) initiatives
- Collaborative [Project Governance](../Project Governance/index.md) frameworks
