# Social and Environmental Impact

## Our Commitment

At Brax, we believe that technology companies have a responsibility to create positive social and environmental impact. Our commitment extends beyond building great products to ensuring our operations and practices contribute to a better world.

## Environmental Impact

### Sustainable Design
- **Longevity**: Devices built to last, reducing electronic waste
- **Repairability**: User-repairable designs with available spare parts
- **Recyclability**: Materials selected for end-of-life recycling

### Carbon Footprint
- **Energy Efficiency**: Low-power components and optimized software
- **Renewable Energy**: Data centers and operations powered by renewables
- **Offsetting**: Carbon offset programs for unavoidable emissions

### E-Waste Reduction
- **Take-Back Program**: Old device recycling and responsible disposal
- **Software Updates**: Extended OS support to prolong device life
- **Refurbishment**: Certified refurbished device programs

## Social Impact

### Digital Rights
- **Privacy by Default**: User privacy as a fundamental right
- **Open Source**: Transparent, auditable software
- **User Control**: Full device ownership and customization

### Accessibility
- **Inclusive Design**: Products accessible to all users
- **Affordability**: Fair pricing and financing options
- **Global Reach**: Support for multiple languages and regions

### Community Development
- **Education**: Free technical training and documentation
- **Employment**: Fair wages and inclusive hiring practices
- **Local Partnerships**: Collaboration with local communities

### Ethical Supply Chain
- **Fair Labor**: Ethical manufacturing practices
- **Conflict-Free Materials**: Responsible sourcing of components
- **Transparency**: Supply chain disclosure and audits
