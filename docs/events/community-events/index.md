# Community Events

Join us for community-organized events, meetups, and gatherings focused on privacy, open source, and the Brax ecosystem.

## About Community Events

Community events are organized by and for the Brax community. These include:

- **Local Meetups**: Connect with fellow Brax users and privacy advocates in your area
- **Online Workshops**: Learn about BraX3, flashing ROMs, and privacy tools
- **Community Calls**: Monthly open calls with the Brax team and community members
- **Hackathons**: Build together and contribute to Brax projects
- **User Groups**: Join topic-specific groups focused on specific OSes or use cases

## Upcoming Community Events

Check back regularly for upcoming community-organized events!

### Monthly Community Call

Join our open community call held on the first Wednesday of each month at 18:00 UTC.

**Topics include:**
- Product updates and roadmap
- Community contributions showcase
- Q&A with the Brax team
- Open discussion

**How to join:** Details announced on our [community forum](https://community.braxtech.net/)

## Organize Your Own Event

Interested in organizing a Brax community event in your area? We'd love to support you!

### What We Can Provide

- Event promotion through our channels
- Branded materials and swag
- Technical speakers (when available)
- Connection to local community members

### Get Started

Email us at [community@braxtech.net](mailto:community@braxtech.net) with:
- Event type and format
- Location and expected attendance
- Date and time
- How we can help
