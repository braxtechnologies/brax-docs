# Company Knowledge Centricity Level

The Company Knowledge Centricity Level is a measure of **how well knowledge is integrated, managed, and utilized within an organization.** It helps to understand the maturity of knowledge management practices and their impact on business performance.\
\
At brax, the Company Knowledge Centricity Level reflects **how integrated and essential knowledge is within our operations**. Currently, different departments are at Grade 2 and Grade 3, meaning we have a solid foundation but still have room for improvement. **Our goal is to reach Grade 5 by next year**, where knowledge is fully centralized, easily accessible, and efficiently shared across the entire organization. This ensures everyone can innovate and perform at their best, contributing to our mission of creating secure, ethical mobile technology.\
