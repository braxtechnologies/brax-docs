# 游 Vision, Mission & Focus

brax is a **product development** and **manufacturing** company. We make **products** that are **good for humanity.** \


brax has grown from a small startup to a leading company in ethical mobile technology. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create products that are not only useful but also uphold the highest standards of privacy and benefit the world. Over the years, we have expanded our reach and improved many lives through our innovative solutions.\
\
Get familiar with our vision, mission, pillars and history here.&#x20;

## Vision

We envision a <mark style="color:blue;">**future**</mark> where <mark style="color:blue;">**every mobile device**</mark> we produce not only <mark style="color:blue;">**enhances digital well-being**</mark> but also embodies th<mark style="color:blue;">**e highest standards of privacy, and data sovereignty and ethical technology.**</mark> By transforming the mobile landscape, we aim to inspire a widespread <mark style="color:blue;">**shift towards more responsible and user-focused technology**</mark> while fostering new sustainable business models.

## Mission

Our mission is to <mark style="color:blue;">**ensure**</mark> that <mark style="color:blue;">**mobile technology benefits**</mark> all of <mark style="color:blue;">**humanity**</mark> by creating products that empower people, foster genuine connections, increase productivity, and enhance quality of life. \
\
By <mark style="color:blue;">**prioritizing ethics**</mark>, we aim to redefine the <mark style="color:blue;">**role of mobile technology**</mark> in society, making it a <mark style="color:blue;">**force for positive change**</mark> and collective advancement.\
\
\

## Pillars

These pillars serve as the bedrock of our endeavors, shaping every decision and innovation we undertake. Rooted in the principles of **ethical technology**, we prioritize transparency, eco-friendliness, and the protection of digital rights in all our endeavors.

Our stringent security protocols are meticulously crafted to shield personal communications and data, while our unwavering commitment to privacy underscores our mission to provide individuals with freedom of choice in the technological realm.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Ethical Technology</mark>

Focuses on transparency through open source, eco-friendly environment, data and digital rights, and freedom of choice.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Top-Level Privacy</mark>

Prioritizes key technologies that protect our mobile devices and ensure the security of personal communications and data from digital attacks.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Respect for Privacy</mark>

Raises awareness and strives to mitigate the prominent risk from Big Tech collecting, analyzing, storing and monetizing on personal data.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Digital Well-being</mark>

Fosters a healthy relationship with technology without harming personal life and interactions.

### <mark style="color:blue;">Equitable Working Practices</mark>

Ensures the implementation of a framework that promotes accountability, security, fulfilment, respect and an ethical workplace to balance workers rights and responsibilities.
