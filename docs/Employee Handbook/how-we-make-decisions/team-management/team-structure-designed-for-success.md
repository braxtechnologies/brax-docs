# Team Structure Designed for Success

Our team structure is carefully **crafted to ensure optimal performance and growth** across all levels.

| Entity         | Goal                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Division**   | Acting as separate entities, divisions are managed by experienced Level 3 or Level 4 managers. Their expertise ensures the smooth functioning of teams and departments, while also providing valuable coaching.     |
| **Department** | Comprising groups of frameworks divided into teams, departments are the backbone of our organization. Led by Level 2 managers, they oversee team growth and maintain essential documentation.                       |
| **Team**       | Teams form the heart of our company. From HR and Operations to various specialized teams, each is managed by a dedicated Level 1 manager, coordinating processes, and a Level 2 manager, driving the team's growth. |
