# 360 Evaluation Matrix

The 360 Evaluation Matrix in brax is a c**omprehensive feedback tool** that involves gathering performance data from various sources, including peers, subordinates, supervisors, and self-assessment&#x73;**.** This **holistic approach** **ensures a well-rounded view** of an employee's capabilities, strengths, and areas for improvement, enhancing the accuracy and effectiveness of performance evaluations.
