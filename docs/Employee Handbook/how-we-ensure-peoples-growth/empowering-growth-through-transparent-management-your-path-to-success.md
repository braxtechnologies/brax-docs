### Embracing Turnover with a Growth Mindset

We acknowledge that **turnover is a natural part of the business landscape** and cannot be addressed solely with "golden handcuffs." Instead, we choose to embrace turnover and be transparent about it, ensuring that the remaining employees feel safe and supported. We believe in celebrating the contributions of those who leave on good terms, valuing their positive impact during their time with the company.

To mitigate the impact of turnover on customers, we redesigned the business model to create multi-person teams, reducing dependency on individual contributors. By documenting every process, we ensure knowledge retention within the company, minimizing the loss of expertise when an individual leaves. For managerial roles, we believe in Long-Term Incentives, recognizing their critical role in developing tacit knowledge and fostering high performance within their teams.

### Performence with Purpose

Transparency extends to all aspects of our business, including performance evaluation. We understand that high performance can mean different things for different individuals and companies. That's why we provide a clear development path for all employees. By knowing roles, responsibilities, and growth opportunities, people are empowered to take control of their career trajectory.

This wiki is designed to increase employee freedom while ensuring alignment with the company's mission. By defining clear behavior and knowledge sets that lead to career success and higher compensation, we empower employees to chart their own course to success.
